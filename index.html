Our history

It began with a spilled latte and a shared dream scribbled on a napkin. Three friends, fueled by caffeine and a rebellious spirit against bland websites, decided to ditch their corporate gigs and launch PixelSpace. Their first office was a cramped attic overflowing with quirky inspiration and a perpetually purring cat. They won their first client with a pitch delivered on roller skates. Word spread about their unconventional approach and designs that popped with personality. Now, years later, they've traded the attic for a vibrant studio, but their passion for bold creativity and client collaboration remains. They're still a little bit rebellious, and the cat occasionally visits.
